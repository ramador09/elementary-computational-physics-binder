# Epilogue

Seven volumes built the machinery; this Epilogue turns it on the course itself. It opens
no new subject and introduces no new field — every tool it uses was forged earlier — but it
computes the threads that run through Volumes I–VII and belong to none of them alone. The
Epilogue is a destination, not a victory lap: each notebook is capstone difficulty, and
each answers *what did the course teach?* one level of abstraction higher than the last.

The arc ascends from a thing to a way of thinking:

- **[E.1](oscillator-biography.ipynb) — The Oscillator's Biography.** *Unity of object.* The course's recurring
  protagonist, the harmonic oscillator, honored by having every face it ever wore —
  trajectory, resonance, thermal degree of freedom, quantum ladder, thermal coth, ring
  polymer, Monte Carlo walk — recomputed and connected, each derived from the next as a
  *measured* limit. It ends on a single warmed oscillator's mean-square displacement,
  reached four independent ways and agreeing to a part in $10^5$.
- **[E.2](four-faces-of-the-action.ipynb) — Four Faces of the Action.** *Unity of principle.* One quantity, $e^{iS/\hbar}$
  rotated to $e^{-\beta H}$, shown to generate the whole course, with double-well tunneling
  by instanton as its summit.
- **[E.3](universality.ipynb) — Universality: Why the Details Didn't Matter.** *Unity of behavior.* The course's
  own critical data gathered onto one page, two microscopically different models landing on
  the same exponents, and the renormalization group named as the reason.
- **[E.4](how-we-knew.ipynb) — How We Knew.** *The method, formalized.* The course's working epistemology made
  explicit — the rendezvous method, error budgets, validation gates, the taught-trap
  culture — and the course's very first computation redone under the mature protocol, the
  ring closed in code.

A short prose **[Afterword](afterword.md)** follows [E.4](how-we-knew.ipynb): the course's last page, where it simply speaks.

The Epilogue may cite the optional Coda (7.23–7.25) lightly but never depends on it; it
reads complete for anyone who skipped the gateway. This is the one place the course allows
itself course-level finality — the arc closed in Volume VII, the volume's pages closed with
the Coda, and everything closed here.
